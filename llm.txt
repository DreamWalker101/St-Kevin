# St Kevin's Primary School Hampton Park

> A Christ-centred Catholic primary school in Hampton Park, Victoria, Australia. Part of the Melbourne Archdiocese Catholic Schools (MACS) network.

St Kevin's Primary School (Hampton Park) offers faith-based primary education for students from Prep to Year 6. The school is led by Principal Jason Micallef and serves a diverse multicultural community in Melbourne's south-east. Learning is guided by the values of faith, learning and belonging.
