Our Community

St Kevin's is more than a school. It is a community where families, staff and students walk together in support, friendship and faith.

A Welcoming Community

Everyone is welcome here. Families from many cultures and backgrounds form the heart of our school community. Together we create a place where every family feels a sense of belonging.

Where every family belongs.

Partnerships with Families

Learning works best when school and home walk together. We value strong relationships with families and believe open communication and partnership help every child flourish.

Together for every child.

Celebrating Together

Community grows through shared moments. School events, celebrations and gatherings bring families together and strengthen the sense of connection across our community.

Every season, every story.

Faith in Community

Our faith brings us together. Masses, prayer and parish celebrations are important moments where our community gathers to reflect, celebrate and grow in faith.

Guided by Christ, shaped by community.

The St Kevin's Spirit

Something special happens when people care for one another. The kindness, generosity and support shown within our community help create a place where children and families truly feel at home.

A community that truly cares.

A community where every child is known, supported and inspired to flourish
