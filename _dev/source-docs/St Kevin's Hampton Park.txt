	About Us
	

	

	

	

	About St Kevin’s
	St Kevin’s is a Christ-centred Catholic primary school where every child is known, valued and supported to flourish. In our diverse Hampton Park community, faith, learning and belonging come together to shape young people who grow in confidence, compassion and purpose.
	

	Completed
	Completed
	Our Story
	St Kevin’s has grown alongside the families of Hampton Park since 1988, becoming a vibrant and culturally rich community where many traditions and languages are celebrated. Our story continues to be shaped by the partnership between students, families, parish and staff who share a commitment to learning and faith.
	

	Completed
	Completed
	Our Mission
	We believe education is sacred work. Our mission is to nurture minds, hearts and spirits so that every child can grow into a person who lives with purpose, compassion and responsibility.
	

	Completed
	Completed
	Our Vision
	At St Kevin’s, learning prepares children not just for the next stage of schooling, but for life. We aim to develop curious, confident and compassionate learners who will contribute positively to their communities and the wider world.
	

	Completed
	Completed
	Our Principal
	Welcome to St Kevin’s. Our school is a place where children are supported to grow academically, socially and spiritually, surrounded by a community that believes deeply in their potential.
What I value most about our school is the way people care for one another. When families, teachers and students work together with trust and purpose, remarkable things happen.

	Our People
	Our staff share a deep commitment to the growth and wellbeing of every child. Working together across Foundation (Prep), Junior (Gr 1 & 2), Middle (Gr 3 & 4) and Senior levels (Gr 5 & 6), our teachers and support staff create learning environments where students feel safe, challenged and inspired.


Foundation Team Photo or video of team smiling and waving
Our Foundation team welcomes children into their first year of school with warmth, patience and a deep understanding of early learning. They create safe and joyful classrooms where curiosity, confidence and independence begin to grow.


Things you might hear them say:
“Let’s try that again together.”
“You can do hard things.”
“Who would like to share their thinking?”


Photo idea: teacher kneeling beside a child writing their first sentence.
Caption under image: First stories. First friendships. First days of school.


Junior Team (Years 1 & 2) Photo or video of team smiling and waving
Our Junior team helps students build strong foundations in literacy, numeracy and learning habits that will carry them through their schooling. These classrooms are full of questions, discovery and the excitement that comes when children begin to realise what they are capable of.


Things you might hear them say:
“Tell me how you figured that out.”
“That’s a great strategy.”
“Who noticed something interesting?”


Photo idea: children working together on a maths task
Caption under image: The moment when learning starts to click.


Middle Team (Years 3 & 4) Photo or video of team smiling and waving
The Middle team supports students as their confidence and independence grow. Learning becomes deeper and more collaborative as students explore ideas, ask thoughtful questions and begin to see themselves as capable learners.


Things you might hear them say:
“Convince me.”
“What makes you think that?”
“Let’s dig a little deeper.”


Photo idea: students discussing ideas around a table
Caption under image: Questions get bigger. Thinking gets deeper.


Senior Team (Years 5 & 6) Photo or video of team smiling and waving
Our Senior team prepares students for the transition to secondary school while nurturing leadership, responsibility and strong learning habits. Students are challenged to think critically, take ownership of their learning and contribute positively to the school community.


Things you might hear them say:
“Show me your reasoning.”
“What could you try next?”
“How can we make this even better?”


Photo idea: students presenting or leading something
Caption under image: Growing into confident learners and leaders.


Specialist Teachers Photo or video of team smiling and waving
Our Specialist teachers bring creativity, movement and new ways of thinking into the learning experience. Through Physical Education, Performing Arts, Visual Arts, STEM, Languages and Critical & Creative Thinking, students discover new talents, interests and ways to express themselves.


Things you might hear them say:
“Let’s give it a go.”
“There’s more than one way to solve that.”
“Who’s ready to perform, build, create or explore?”


Photo idea: students performing, painting, building or playing sport
Caption under image: Creativity, movement and discovery.


Support Staff Photo or video of team smiling and waving
Behind every great school is a team of people working together to support students and families. Our leaders, coaches, administration team, library staff and intervention specialists help ensure every child is known, supported and able to thrive.


Things you might hear them say:
“How can I help?”
“Let’s work this out together.”
“Everyone belongs here.”


Photo idea: admin welcoming families or staff supporting a student
Caption under image: The people who make everything work.
