Welcome to St Kevin's

A community where every child is known, supported and inspired to flourish

Belonging

Belonging sits at the heart of life at St Kevin's. When children feel known, safe and valued, they grow in confidence, curiosity and courage to learn.

Every child matters here.

Learning with purpose

Learning at St Kevin's is both structured and meaningful, grounded in evidence-informed teaching and rich opportunities for inquiry. We form learners who think deeply and engage with the world around them.

Clear teaching. Curious minds. Deep thinking.

Faith in action

Our Catholic faith shapes the spirit of our school and the way we care for one another. Through prayer, service and daily action, students learn to live the Gospel values of respect, compassion and love.

Guided by Christ in all we do.

Growing together

St Kevin's is a welcoming and diverse community where families, staff and students work together. It is this partnership that creates the warmth, trust and shared purpose that define our school.

Together we shape the future.

The St Kevin's Effect

Over time, something special happens here. Through strong relationships, high expectations and genuine care, children grow into confident and compassionate young people.
