# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

Parents of primary-school-aged children (5–12) in Hampton Park and surrounds, researching Catholic education options for their child. Also prospective families new to the area, and current school families checking news, events, and contact info. Mobile-first: most visits happen on a phone between school pickup and dinner.

## Product Purpose

St Kevin's Primary School public website. Replaces an existing WordPress site. The site communicates the school's identity, Catholic mission, learning programs, and community warmth — and converts interested families into enrolment enquiries. Success: a visitor lands, feels the school's character, and contacts the school or downloads an enrolment pack.

## Brand Personality

Warm, grounded, purposeful. A school that takes faith and learning seriously without being stiff. The brand voice is: trusted, welcoming, quietly proud.

## Anti-references

- Generic WordPress school template aesthetics (clip-art icons, table layouts, accordion FAQs in navy boxes)
- Overproduced corporate Catholic school sites (stock photography of diverse children in posed rows, excessive gold/cream)
- Cold institutional government-school sites (flat grey, no imagery, bureaucratic language)
- SaaS-cream landing page aesthetics (feature-card grids, "streamline your learning journey" copy, big emoji)

## Design Principles

1. **Mission before marketing.** Every section earns its place by communicating something real about the school — its faith, its community, its learning. No filler copy.
2. **Warmth through specificity.** Warmth isn't achieved by using cream backgrounds or round avatars. It comes from real imagery, real names, real stories, real copy about this specific school in Hampton Park.
3. **Editorial restraint.** A small number of sections done with conviction beats a long page of generic sections. Less is more; each section should be worth scrolling to.
4. **Mobile is the primary canvas.** Hampton Park parents check this site on the school pickup queue. Design for a phone in one hand, not a desktop presentation.
5. **Accessible by default.** Catholic school communities include families with diverse accessibility needs. WCAG AA minimum. Readable contrast. No motion-gating of content.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA. All animations respect `prefers-reduced-motion`. Sidebar navigation keyboard-accessible (focus trap, Escape to close). Search input accessible with label. No content hidden behind JS-only interactions.
